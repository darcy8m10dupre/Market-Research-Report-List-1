<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humidification devices market includes various types that enhance indoor air quality. Vapor type humidifiers work by heating water to produce steam, which is then cooled and released into the air, effectively increasing humidity levels. In contrast, water spray humidifiers utilize a fine mist or spray of water to directly humidify the air, often requiring less energy. Both types cater to different needs, with vapor humidifiers typically providing warmer moisture, while water spray humidifiers offer a cooler alternative.</p></p>
