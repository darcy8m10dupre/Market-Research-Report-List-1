<p><ul><li>Electroplating</li><li>Electroless Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal plating market encompasses two primary types: electroplating and electroless plating. Electroplating involves the use of an electric current to deposit a metal coating onto a substrate, enhancing properties like corrosion resistance and appearance. In contrast, electroless plating relies on a chemical reduction process that does not require an electric current, allowing for uniform coatings on complex shapes. Both methods serve various industries, including automotive, electronics, and aerospace, ensuring durability and performance in metal components.</p></p>
