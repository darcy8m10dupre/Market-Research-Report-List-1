<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial CMOS image sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Front-Side Illumination (FSI) and Back-Side Illumination (BSI). FSI sensors capture light through the front surface, making them simpler and cost-effective but with lower light sensitivity. In contrast, BSI sensors have a structure that allows light to reach the photodiodes from the back, enhancing light sensitivity and image quality, particularly in low-light environments. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial applications, emphasizing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
