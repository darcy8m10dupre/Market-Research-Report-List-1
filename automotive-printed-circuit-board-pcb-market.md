<p><p>The automotive PCB market comprises three primary types: Single-Sided PCBs feature components on one side and are typically used in simpler applications where space and complexity are limited. Double-Sided PCBs allow components on both sides, accommodating more complex circuits and higher functionality while maintaining a compact design. Multi-Layer PCBs consist of multiple layers of circuitry, enabling high-density integration, advanced features, and improved performance, making them ideal for modern vehicles with sophisticated systems like infotainment and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
