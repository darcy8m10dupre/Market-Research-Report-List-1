<p><p>The Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) optical film market comprises several key types: reflective film, brightness enhancement film, and diffusion film. Reflective film is designed to enhance the visibility of displays by reflecting ambient light, making screens brighter. Brightness enhancement film intensifies light output from the backlight, improving image brightness and clarity. Diffusion film evenly distributes light across the screen, reducing hotspots and enhancing viewing angles. Together, these films optimize display performance and are crucial in various applications, including televisions, monitors, and mobile devices.</p></p>
