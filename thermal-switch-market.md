<p><strong>The Thermal Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open Thermal Switch</li><li>Normally Closed Thermal Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal switch market comprises two main types: normally open and normally closed thermal switches. Normally open thermal switches allow current to flow only when a specified temperature is reached, commonly used in applications requiring activation at high temperatures, such as in HVAC systems. Conversely, normally closed thermal switches conduct electricity until a designated temperature is reached, shutting off the circuit to prevent overheating, often found in appliances and motors. These switches are essential for temperature regulation and safety in various industries.</p></p>
