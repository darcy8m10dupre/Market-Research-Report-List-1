<p><p>Semiconductor Dielectric Etching Equipment (SDEE) includes tools used to remove dielectric materials during semiconductor manufacturing. The market for this equipment is divided into two main types: wet etching and dry etching. Wet etching involves chemical solutions to dissolve materials, offering high selectivity and uniformity for removing thin films. In contrast, dry etching utilizes plasma or gas to etch materials, providing better precision and control, making it essential for complex circuit designs. Both types are crucial for advancing semiconductor technologies.</p></p>
