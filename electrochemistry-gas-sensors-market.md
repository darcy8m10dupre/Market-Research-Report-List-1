<p><p>Electrochemistry gas sensors are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I sensors typically utilize a single electrochemical cell to detect gases, providing high sensitivity and rapid response times for applications such as air quality monitoring and industrial safety. Type II sensors, on the other hand, incorporate multiple electrodes or advanced configurations to improve accuracy and selectivity for specific gases, making them suitable for complex environments like chemical processing and environmental analysis. Both types play crucial roles in safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
