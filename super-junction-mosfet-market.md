<p><p>The Super-Junction MOSFET market is primarily categorized into multiple-epitaxy and deep-trench types. Multiple-epitaxy involves layering various semiconductor materials to enhance efficiency and reduce switching losses, making it ideal for high-power applications. On the other hand, the deep-trench technology employs a trench structure to achieve a higher breakdown voltage and improved thermal performance, offering better space utilization. Both types contribute to advancements in power electronics, catering to the growing demand for energy-efficient devices across various industries.</p></p>
