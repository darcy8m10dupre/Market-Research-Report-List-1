<p><strong>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food mercury analysis systems are essential for detecting mercury levels in food products to ensure safety and compliance. The Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type utilizes a cold vapor generation technique to measure mercury concentration by quantifying absorbed light at specific wavelengths. In contrast, the Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type employs atomic fluorescence spectroscopy, where mercury emits light when excited, allowing for highly sensitive detection. Both systems are crucial for monitoring and regulating mercury content in food products, ensuring public health protection.</p></p>
